Pupusas

Pupusas are traditional Salvadoran stuffed flatbreads, typically filled with beans, cheese, or pork. They are served with curtido, a pickled cabbage slaw, and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour mass
  • 1 Â½ cups warm water
  • 1 cup refried beans (or other filling: cheese, pork)
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • Salt to taste

How to cook it

  1. Mix the flour mass and warm water to form a soft dough. Add salt.
  2. Divide dough into balls, flatten each ball, and place filling in the center.
  3. Fold and seal, then flatten again into discs.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until both sides are golden and slightly crispy.
